5 Examples of Car Dashboard & Display


Pros vs Cons with the current designs and Why it is good vs bad?


2013 MERCEDES BENZ C250

DASHBPARD:
Pros: The simplicity is clear and elegant, the fonts are bold and easy to read. It also has extra shields around each element of the dashboard which enhances the visibility.
Cons: Even though the fuel and temperature indicators are separated when the dashboard is looked at, the gauge lines for the fuel gauge continues which makes it a little confusing. The speedometer does not reflect in both miles/hr and km/hr .

LCD DISPLAY:
Pros: It is functional and easy to use.
Cons: The design is rather basic and unimaginative.

2017 HONDA ACCORD

DASHBOARD:
Pros: The fuel and Temperature indicators are clearly distinct and the speedometer is in both miles/hr and Km/hr unlike the Mercedes Benz E250. The design is also simple and clear
Cons: The font for the Km/hr is not very bold and clear. The fault indicators are also not very distinct on the dashboard.

LCD DISPLAY:
Pros: It does have a lot of functionalities. There are so many things you can do on it including accessing real time information about the car.
Cons: There are 2 LCD displays which is redundant. One is for the entertainment system which includes the radio, Bluetooth etc. While the other gives real-time info about the car and how far it can go, shows compass, and it also gives an interface to get information about the car and the current journey such as fuel mileage, direction of travel, etc. One LCD should have been enough for those purposes.

20O9 AUDI A14:

DASHBOARD:

2016 CHEVY SUBURBAN:

DASHBOARD
Pros: This dashboard shows the state of the battery and the oil, in addition to the temperature and fuel gauge. All of these indicators are prominent and separated from each other and so there is no confusion
Cons: The speedometer should reflect in both miles/hr and Km/hr. the edges of the designs are to sharp. A little bit of roundness around the edges would make the design easy on the eye.

Most common features of the dashboard

Speedometer, tachometer, fuel gauge, fault status/alarms, engine temperature, oil pressure gauge, indicators for headlamps, brakes, Trafficators, cruise control, blind spot monitor, temperature of the environment, number of miles used, time, seat belt indicator, car door opening.

Most common features of the LCD Display

Stream music and play movies, Use Bluetooth and wifi, operate phones and other devices hands-free, easily navigate maps and the roads, get information about the car settings, Get more information about faults.

Rare uses of a car dashboard.

Some alarm indicators e.g drowsiness detector and heads up display.

Rare uses of a car LCD display.
  • To access useful driving data.
  • To access and modify customizable vehicle settings

List the controls that the user interacts with

The car dashboard:

The trafficator that shows where to turn (left/right), headlamp, cruise control, gear change, opening and closing a door, seatbelts, speedometer, the gas tank.

The LCD display.

The volume knobs, the entertainment system, the map, the alarm indicators, the car manual.

What are some common mistakes you can make with this current designs?

The car dashboard:

The users sometimes do not know the meaning of some alarm indicators and sometimes the car would have brokendown before they realize what it means. For example, the tire pressure light or the traction control light.

The LCD display.

The users sometimes do not know how to use some features on the LCD or know how it works. Also some of the icons are not recognizable.

Suggestions and Improvements

  • The engine temperature and fuel guage indicators should be prominent.
  • It should display the number of miles left before the car runs out of gas.
  • The icons on the LCD display should be easily understood and recognizable
